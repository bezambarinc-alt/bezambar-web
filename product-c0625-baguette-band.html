C0625

Baguette Band
Oval Eternity Ring

Price Upon Request

Inquire Now Contact an Advisor

A masterwork in architectural precision. The C0625 Baguette Band is an oval eternity ring composed entirely of baguette-cut diamonds, set side by side in a seamless channel — like luminous bricks of fire encircling the finger without interruption.


Each baguette is individually selected by Bez Ambar for its color uniformity and step-cut clarity — stones that reveal their inner architecture under light, glowing with a cool, architectural intensity that stands apart from brilliant-cut designs.


The oval silhouette elongates the hand with effortless elegance. Set in 18k gold, the band carries the Bez Ambar signature of perfect fit and refined finish. Every stone is set flush, every edge crisp. A band that commands attention without asking for it.

Style: C0625
Cut: Baguette-cut diamonds, channel-set
Setting: Seamless channel, oval silhouette
Metal: 18k Gold (white, yellow, or rose — available upon request)
Finish: High polish
Handcrafted in Los Angeles
Signed by Bez Ambar
