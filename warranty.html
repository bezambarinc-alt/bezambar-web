Warranty & Policies

Quality craftsmanship backed by our commitment to excellence

Lifetime Warranty

We Stand Behind Every Piece

Bez Ambar jewelry comes with a comprehensive lifetime warranty on craftsmanship and materials.

Every piece of Bez Ambar jewelry is protected by our lifetime warranty, reflecting our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship and quality materials.

Coverage Period

  • 12 Months Full Coverage: Covers all defects in materials and craftsmanship from the purchase date
  • Ongoing Coverage: After the initial 12 months, warranty continues for the lifetime of the original owner with annual inspection at an authorized Bez Ambar service center

What Is Covered

  • Defects in materials and craftsmanship
  • Breakage of stones or prongs due to manufacturing defects
  • Repairs and restoration to original condition

What Is Not Covered

  • Damage from accidents, misuse, or negligence
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear, including surface scratches and patina
  • Loss or theft
  • Damage from improper care or storage
  • Chemical or environmental damage

Warranty Terms

This warranty applies exclusively to the original purchaser and is non-transferable. To maintain warranty coverage after 12 months, your jewelry must be inspected annually at an authorized Bez Ambar service center. We recommend professional cleaning and inspection at least once per year to ensure your piece remains in optimal condition.
