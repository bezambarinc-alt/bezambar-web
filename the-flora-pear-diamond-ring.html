The Flora

18 Carat D Flawless Pear Diamond

Price Upon Request

The Flora is the ultimate expression of Bez Ambar's mastery — an extraordinary 18-carat D Flawless pear-shaped diamond, set in a one-of-a-kind mounting that elevates the stone to a realm beyond jewelry.

The mounting features a floral motif of precisely cut marquise diamonds that cradle the center stone from beneath, supporting both its structural integrity and its visual drama. Each marquise petal radiates outward like a bloom in full flower, amplifying the fire of the center diamond while ensuring it sits with perfect security and balance.
