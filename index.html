The Master

The inventor of the
Princess Cut diamond.

In 1982, Bez Ambar unveiled the Quadrillion® — the square-cut diamond the world would come to know as the Princess Cut. Today it is the most popular diamond cut on earth, found on tens of millions of engagement rings worldwide.

Bez never stopped innovating. The Blaze® cut. The Divine Cut®. The Micro-Pavé setting. Six patented innovations, each one changing the industry. He is still here — still cutting, still creating — one piece at a time, by hand, in his Los Angeles atelier.
