How Bespoke Works

+

Our bespoke process begins with a personal consultation — in person at our Los Angeles atelier or by phone. We'll discuss your family's story, select gemstones together, and create a design that exists nowhere else. From concept to completion, the process typically takes 3–4 weeks.

About the Stones

+

Bez Ambar selects only natural, untreated gemstones of exceptional quality. Diamonds are GIA-certified, with a minimum color grade of G and clarity of VS2. Each birthstone is hand-selected to complement the overall design.

Care Instructions

+

Fine jewelry requires gentle care. Clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid exposure to chemicals, perfume, and chlorinated water. Store individually in the provided Bez Ambar case. We offer complimentary cleaning and inspection at our Los Angeles atelier.

The Inventor

Bez Ambar — Creator of the Princess Cut

In 1979, Bez Ambar invented the Princess cut — a diamond shape that would become one of the most popular in the world. From his atelier in Los Angeles, he continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in diamond design.

The Blaze and Alara cuts followed — proprietary techniques that no other house can replicate. Every piece bearing the Bez Ambar name carries decades of innovation and an uncompromising commitment to craft.

When you choose Bez Ambar, you're not just choosing jewelry. You're choosing a piece of diamond history.
